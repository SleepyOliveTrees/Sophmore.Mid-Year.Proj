About Us!

OYATaskFlow is a website that helps you organize your tasks! You can organize your tasks on our built-in calendar to-do list. We also have a feature that calculates your total cash flow. For instance, when you gain money, you can input that into the calculator and when you spend money, you can update the calculator accordingly. THe number at the top of the screen will update with every input you add. Look at our tutorial video below for help on how to use the website!
